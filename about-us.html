Bonnie Acuña

I am freelance full stack web developer trying to make my path in the world. I have never been tech savvy and tried to rebel against using too much tech. But as a stay at home mom with two kids, I've realized that no matter how much I try to fight against it, I had to embrace the fact that tech was everywhere. So I would push my kids into coding and programming thinking they had a chance at making a difference in their lives since coding is the future. I never thought I would ever get into it myself. I ended up taking a coding bootcamp and from there my life changed. It has opened so many doors for me and now I finally feel like I am taking control of my own life.

Melissa Ortiz

I’m a web developer now but back in high school I took one of those career assessment tests and the results were either social worker or detective. I didn’t think I was cut out for detective work so social work it was. After being told I was too young to finish the program and to come back in a few years I decided to take a break and do what most girls my age were doing, having babies. I went back a few years ago and knew that was not what I wanted to do with my life. I started googling different careers and by chance stumbled onto programming and the rest is history.
